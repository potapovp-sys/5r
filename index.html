Architecture Bureau · Moscow · since 2001

About Us

Architecture bureau Fifth Radius was founded in 2001. We provide a complete range of design services for structures of any complexity — from commercial premises to country villas and estates.

Our team comprises leading experts in finishing materials, construction technologies and engineering systems. Our architects work across diverse styles and provide full author supervision, including remote oversight for projects worldwide.

5R + partner. A core principle of the bureau is a genuine partnership with every client. We create unique, fully livable spaces built entirely around each client's vision and wishes.

How We Work · 5 Steps

1
We learn the client's wishes, answer all questions and develop the project concept.
2
We agree on the scope of work, timeline and budget.
3
We create the project, align it with the client and select subcontractors.
4
We provide author supervision throughout the entire construction cycle and monitor material deliveries.
5
We arrange the furniture, hang the curtains and hand over the keys.
