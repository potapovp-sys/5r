Architecture Bureau · since 2001

About Us

Architecture bureau 'Fifth Radius' was founded in 2001. We provide a complete range of services for architectural design of any complexity — from commercial spaces to country villas and estates.

Our team comprises top experts in finishing materials, construction technologies and engineering systems. Our architects work across diverse styles and provide author supervision, including remote oversight for projects worldwide.

5R + partner. Our core principle is a partnership approach with every client. We create unique, fully livable spaces built around each client's vision and wishes.

How We Work · 5 Steps

1
We get to know the client's wishes, answer all questions and develop the project concept.
2
We agree on the scope of work, timeline and budget.
3
We create the project, align it with the client and select subcontractors.
4
We provide author supervision throughout the entire construction cycle and monitor material deliveries.
5
We arrange the furniture, hang the curtains and hand over the keys.
